  • Insights: Enhanced data in the gold layer for advanced analytics and visualization.

Outcome: Streamlined, automated data pipeline for up-to-date cryptocurrency insights.

Geospatial Analysis for Wildfire Prediction in California

About the Project: Leveraged satellite imagery and geospatial techniques to analyze and predict wildfire occurrences in California.

Objectives: Utilize climate data to understand spatial patterns and predict wildfire risks.

Approach:

Outcome: Provided predictive insights for wildfire management and preventive strategies.
